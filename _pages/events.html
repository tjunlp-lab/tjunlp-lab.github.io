- 2025 Events Gallery

On January 13, 2025, Tianjin University hosted the Pre-Launch Meeting and Technical Seminar for the National Key R&D Project "Research on Theories and Key Technologies for Aligning Large Language Models with Pluralistic Values". As the leading institution of the project, Tianjin University has gathered over 30 experts and core team members to discuss the research framework and technical directions.

TJUNLP has achieved remarkable recognition at COLING 2025 held in Abu Dhabi, with Yongqi Leng winning the Best Long Paper Award for his research on multi-task learning in LLMs, while postdoctoral researcher Yuqi Ren, PhD student Renren Jin, Professor Tongxuan Zhang from Tianjin Normal University have received the Outstanding Paper Award for their study on how LLMs mirror cognitive language processing, showcasing their outstanding contributions to NLP and AI interpretability.


- 2024 Events Gallery

On the 19th of January 2024, the Yunnan Provincial Key R&D Program Seminar at Tianjin University highlighted advancements in large-scale neural machine translation models centered on Chinese, exploring multilingual translation, low-resource language solutions, and industrial applications to support digital economy needs.

At LREC-COLING 2024 in Turin, Italy, TJUNLP's scholars shone with nine accepted papers, including a Best Paper nomination, as they shared groundbreaking research in NLP and fostered rich global academic collaborations amidst the vibrant setting of one of the field's premier conferences.

The launch meeting of the "China-Portugal Joint Laboratory of Natural Language Processing " under the national key R&D program "Intergovernmental International Science and Technology Innovation Cooperation" was held at Tianjin University on July 9, 2024.

Professor Xiong Deyi visited the Faculty of Sciences at the University of Lisbon in September 2024, upon the invitation of Professor António Branco, to further strengthen the cooperation between TJUNLP and the NLX Lab in areas such as innovative talent development, cutting-edge technology research, and project collaboration, highlighted by the unveiling of the Sino-Portugal Joint Laboratory of Natural Language Processing.

Dr. Yuqi Ren successfully defended his dissertation titled "Research on Enhancing Natural Language Processing Models and Their Interpretability Based on Cognitive Processing Signals," marking a significant milestone in his academic journey.

The first ever international student at TJUNLP: Supryadi from Indonesia presented the paper "Empirical Study on Translating Toxic Sentences with Large Language Models vs. Machine Translation" at the International Conference on Intelligent Multilingual Information Processing (IMLIP) 2024, held on November 16-17, 2024.

Dr. Liu Chuang successfully defended his dissertation titled "Discipline Knowledge Evaluation for Chinese Large Language Models" marking a significant milestone in his academic journey.
